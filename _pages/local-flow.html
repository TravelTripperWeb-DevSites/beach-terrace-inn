Farmer’s Markets

Coastal San Diego County offers a variety of weekly certified Farmer’s Markets featuring fresh, locally sourced products.

  • Wednesdays in Carlsbad between 1-5p
  • Thursdays in Oceanside between 9a-1p and then again from 5p-9p
  • Saturdays in La Costa between 10a-2p
  • Sundays in Encinitas between 10a-2p
