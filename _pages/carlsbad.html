Carlsbad is a place for renewal and relaxation - and it always has been. What started as a small resort town with historic Carlsbad Mineral Waters has now grown to one of the largest cities in San Diego County. Luckily, we still have that kick-back vibe, undulating from our rolling waves, vast stretches of coastline, plunging cliffs, and stunning sunsets.
